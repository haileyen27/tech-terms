    • Summary

    The Walker Pool is a room, formerly a swimming pool, located in the basement of the Walker Arts and Humanities Center

    Details

    The Walker Pool is an empty swimming pool located in the basement of Walker. It is not accessible to students. It is currently used for the VPA Department's prop storage. The pool is left over from the former Sherman Gym.

    History

    1946-1949 - Sherman Gym built
    1980 - SDC built, replacing Sherman Gym
    1980s - Sherman Gym building remodeled
    1985 (August 2) - Walker Arts and Humanities Center opened (pool closed off)
