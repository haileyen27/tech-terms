    Econo, otherwise known as Econofoods is a name for the grocery store Tadych's Marketplace.

    Details

    The name came from shortening the name for the establishment. Up until July of 2022, Econofoods was a 24 hours grocery store that many students would visit in the early hours of the morning for anything from baked goods, energy drinks, to alcohol. By September of 2022, Econofoods had been sold to Coborn's, a different chain of stores. that pulled the plug on it's 24 hour service.
